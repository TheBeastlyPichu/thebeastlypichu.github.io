Music Commissions

Details:

❥ To commission me, email me at thebeastlypichu@gmail.com
❥ I will try to get your commission done within 6 weeks of ordering
❥ I will send work-in-progress renders of your track and listen to
any feedback you provide
❥ I may not always be open for commissions, so just send me an email
and I'll let you know if I have commissions open
❥ I only take one commission at a time; if I'm busy with someone
else's commission, I'll try to let you know when I'm done with
theirs so you can commission me afterwards
❥ You can only commission one track at a time, although I'm open
to discussing paid work on larger projects
❥ I'd generally charge around $75 per minute of music, although
this depends on the complexity of the track
❥ I require you to pay 50% upfront and the other 50% before
receiving the final lossless copy of the track
❥ I'd prefer you pay with PayPal

What I Can Do:

❥ Original compositions
❥ Covers/arrangements/remixes
❥ High quality rips in the vein of SiIvaGunner (whether I can make
a rip concept depends on a variety of factors, so I may have to turn
down your rip commission)
❥ Experimental electronic
❥ “Real” breakcore (not the Sewerslvt/Goreshit/Machine Girl-style
jungle that’s been labelled as breakcore by people on TikTok,
although I'd probably be able to make that too)
❥ Chiptune/8-bit, including Gameboy tracks made using LSDJ
and recorded off my original Gameboy (model DMG-01), and
hardware-playable SNES/SPC700 tracks
❥ Non-chiptune video game music (like the stuff you’d here in
Undertale or other indie games)
❥ Vocals using Synth V (as of right now, the only paid voicebank
I own is Natalie, although I can also use any of the lite
voicebanks)

What I Can't Do:

❥ Orchestral scores
❥ Mainstream EDM (e.g., dubstep, house, and trap)
❥ Any genre of hip-hop
❥ Anything using live instruments
❥ Real, non-synthetic vocals
❥ Any synthesized vocals that you don't provide the lyrics for
❥ Anything using hardware or software that I don't own
