About Me

Hobbies/Interests:

My hobbies/interests include high quality rips,
synthesizers, music production, baking, and art.

Fandoms:

My fandoms include SiIvaGunner, Undertale/Deltarune,
Homestuck, Hamilton, Firefly, Star Wars, and Star Trek.

Ripping:

I started ripping during in 2020 during quarantine, and I got my
first rip published to TimmyTurnersGrandDad in April 2021.
Later that year in August, I got my first rip accepted to
SiIvaGunner. I kept making and submitting rips to various
ripping channels, and in October 2022, I was finally invited
to a ripping team for the first time, that being TTGD.
Eventually, I also got invited to the SiIvaGunner team on Christmas 2023.

While I'm not the most prolific ripper, I still enjoy making rips when
I get the chance. My favorite types of rips to make are arrangement rips.
I have made several of these, and my favorite one that I have made is easily
Bad Apple!! feat.nomico - Touhou Danmaku Kagura Phantasia Lost.

Music Production:

I started producing music around the same time that I started ripping.
I enjoy producing many genres, including VGM, chiptune,
breakcore, and experimental electronic.
